Why Door County?

One Peninsula, Endless Possibilities

"Door County" refers to an area comprised of more than 3,000 square miles, 35 named islands, thousands of diverse businesses, and countless ways to spend a day, week, or an entire season. Explore further to learn why Door County is one of the top vacation spots in the US and start packing your bags for a Midwestern getaway unlike any other.
